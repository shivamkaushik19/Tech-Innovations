What is Social Media Marketing?

Social media marketing is a digital method used by businesses to connect with customers, earn brand awareness, and easily promote services and products where users are spending most of their time: scrolling through their feeds. By being active on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest, you’re making your business more approachable and accessible to a wider audience, while also maintaining a good social presence—a vital part of your digital success.

Despite what you may think, social media marketing does not mean being on every social network that exists. Like we said, it’s all about what works for your brand. Thinking about the story you want to tell, your brand image, and where you think you can connect best with your target audience will help lead you to the social media platforms that make sense for your business—could be one, could be three! Influencer Marketing: All You Need to Know to Plan, Execute, and Generate ROI

Influencer marketing is the process of collaborating with influencers in order to expand the reach and awareness of your brand. The right investment can help you cement your position in the market and build trust and credibility with your audience. When done well, a successful influencer marketing campaign can work wonders to boost the bottom line of your business. However, in order to ensure a good Return on Investment (ROI), there are a number of things that you need to keep in mind.

This guide will help you build a campaign that sets you up for success by looking at:

  • What is influencer marketing and how it works.
  • The steps to building an effective strategy.
  • Best practices to help you increase your ROI.
  • How to streamline your processes with an influencer marketing platform.

What Is Influencer Marketing?

Influencer marketing is a form of social media marketing that involves collaborating with professional influencers who publicly endorse your brand. This helps you build your business and reach a larger audience.

Before we look at the different types of influencers that you can hire, let’s begin by taking a look at the benefits of embracing this effective marketing strategy.

Benefits of Influencer Marketing

Here are the biggest benefits of using an influencer marketing strategy to boost your brand:

  • Build trust: Influencers have already built trust, engagement, and credibility with their followers, so their audience is more likely to buy into their content and recommendations.
  • Improve brand awareness: The right strategy can expand your reach and online positioning, encouraging more people to find out who you are and what you have to offer.
  • Enrich your content strategy: An experienced influencer can provide you with inspiration for new content ideas, helping to fill the gaps in your content strategy.
  • Reach your target audience: Relevant influencers can help you share your content with a niche audience that is more likely to be interested in your product.

Advertise to Your Exact Audience

Target the person, not the keyword

Paid advertising campaigns on social networks offer distinct advantages compared to traditional pay-per-click (PPC) on traditional search engines.

Social media advertising allows you to isolate people with specific jobs, skills, associations, age, gender, interests, hobbies, locations, and much more.
